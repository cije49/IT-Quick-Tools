Privacy Policy

IT Quick Tools does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All features of the app (including IP calculator, port checker, ping tool, WiFi QR generator, and speed test) operate locally on the device or perform direct network requests without collecting user-identifiable information.

Network Usage:

Third-party services:

Security:
